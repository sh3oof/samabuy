SamaBuy FZ-LLC ("SamaBuy", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy complies with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 (PDPL) and all applicable VARA regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, nationality, Emirates ID or passport number
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, residential address
  • Financial data: Transaction history, wallet addresses, payment details (processed by MoonPay)
  • Device data: IP address, device type, operating system, app usage data
  • Biometric data: Face ID or fingerprint (stored locally on your device only — never sent to our servers)
  • Zakat data: Portfolio values used for Zakat calculations (stored locally)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your SamaBuy account
  • To process cryptocurrency transactions via MoonPay
  • To conduct KYC and AML compliance checks as required by UAE law
  • To calculate and track your Zakat obligations
  • To send transaction confirmations and important notifications
  • To improve our app through anonymised analytics
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Data Sharing

We share your data only with trusted partners necessary to provide our services:

  • MoonPay: For payment processing and KYC verification
  • Regulatory authorities: When required by UAE law, VARA regulations, or court order
  • Fraud prevention services: To protect against financial crime

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Biometric Data

SamaBuy uses Face ID (iOS) and Fingerprint (Android) for convenient login. This biometric data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your device using the device's secure enclave
  • Never transmitted to SamaBuy's servers
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Controlled entirely by you and your device settings

5. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication requirements
  • Strict access controls for our team members

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Duration of account plus 5 years after closure
  • Transaction records: 8 years as required by UAE AML regulations
  • KYC documents: 5 years after account closure
  • Device and usage data: 2 years

7. Your Rights

Under UAE PDPL, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to withdraw consent for non-essential data processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights: privacy@samabuy.com

8. Cookies and Analytics

SamaBuy uses anonymised analytics to improve our services. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

9. Children's Privacy

SamaBuy is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed by MoonPay in countries outside the UAE. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place for all such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email.
