Key Risks

Market Volatility
High Risk
Cryptocurrency prices can fluctuate dramatically within short periods. Assets can lose 50% or more of their value rapidly.
🔒
Security Risks
High Risk
Digital assets can be lost due to cyberattacks, hacking, or loss of access credentials. While SamaBuy implements strong security measures, no system is completely immune.
⚖️

Medium Risk
Cryptocurrency regulations are evolving globally. Changes in UAE or international regulations could affect your ability to trade or access your assets.

1. No Financial Advice

SamaBuy does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Nothing in this app or on our website should be construed as a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any cryptocurrency.

We strongly recommend consulting a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.

2. Capital at Risk

You should never invest more than you can afford to lose. Consider the following before trading:

  • Only invest discretionary funds — money you do not need for living expenses
  • Diversify your investments — do not put all funds into cryptocurrency
  • Have a clear investment strategy and stick to it
  • Consider your personal financial situation and risk tolerance
  • Be aware of the tax implications in your jurisdiction

3. Sharia and Financial Risk

While SamaBuy ensures Sharia compliance of listed assets, Sharia compliance does not eliminate financial risk. Halal assets can still lose significant value. The Sharia screening process addresses religious permissibility, not financial performance.

4. Transaction Irreversibility

Cryptocurrency transactions on the blockchain are generally irreversible once confirmed. SamaBuy cannot reverse completed transactions. Please double-check all transaction details before confirming.

  • Always verify wallet addresses before sending
  • Ensure you are sending the correct cryptocurrency on the correct network
  • Start with small test transactions when sending to new addresses
