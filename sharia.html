SamaBuy is built on the foundation of Islamic finance principles. Every feature, asset, and transaction is designed to comply with Sharia law.

1. Our Sharia Commitment

SamaBuy operates as the UAE's first dedicated Sharia-compliant cryptocurrency brokerage platform. Our operations are guided by three core Islamic finance prohibitions:

  • Riba (ربا) — No interest-based transactions. SamaBuy does not charge or pay interest on any account or transaction.
  • Gharar (غرر) — No excessive uncertainty. All transactions are transparent with clear terms, pricing, and outcomes.
  • Maysir (ميسر) — No gambling or speculation. SamaBuy does not offer leveraged trading, derivatives, or speculative instruments.

2. Sharia Supervisory Board

SamaBuy maintains a Sharia Supervisory Board (SSB) comprising qualified Islamic finance scholars. The SSB is responsible for:

  • Reviewing and certifying all listed digital assets
  • Issuing Sharia compliance certificates (fatwas) for SamaBuy's operations
  • Reviewing new features and products before launch
  • Publishing annual Sharia compliance reports
  • Providing guidance on Zakat calculations for digital assets

All SSB opinions are published in both Arabic and English for full transparency.

3. Asset Screening Methodology

Every cryptocurrency listed on SamaBuy undergoes a rigorous Sharia screening process:

  • Primary business activity must be halal and beneficial to society
  • The asset must have clear utility or value beyond speculation
  • No involvement in prohibited industries
  • No interest-bearing mechanisms built into the protocol
  • Sufficient transparency in operations and governance

5. Zakat on Cryptocurrency

SamaBuy includes a built-in Zakat calculator based on the following scholarly methodology:

  • Cryptocurrencies held for one lunar year (Hawl) are subject to Zakat
  • Zakat is calculated at 2.5% of the current market value
  • The Nisab threshold is based on the current gold price (85 grams of gold)
  • Only assets exceeding the Nisab are subject to Zakat

6. Purification (Tazkiyah)

If a user inadvertently receives non-Sharia-compliant assets, SamaBuy recommends:

  • Immediately converting the asset to a Sharia-compliant alternative
  • Donating any impermissible gains to charity (sadaqah)
  • Consulting a qualified Islamic scholar for personal guidance

7. No Riba Guarantee

SamaBuy guarantees that:

  • No interest is charged on any account balance
  • No interest-bearing savings or lending products are offered
  • Transaction fees are transparent spread markups, not interest
  • No margin trading or leveraged products are available
